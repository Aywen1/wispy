Transcendance is a POLYvalant, TRANScending game engine that is more or less an abstraction layer to LibGDX, and allow you to ACE your projects.

The engine itself is free and open source, but you are free to use the engine for anything as long as :

>you dont rebrand it and make people pay for it
>disclose that you use Transcendance in your project
>that your project respect the licence of the engine, and the libraries used
